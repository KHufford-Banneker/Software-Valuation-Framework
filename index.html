Software Valuation Framework

Enterprise software valuations are driven by the Rule of 40 (ARR Growth + EBITDA Margin), then adjusted for company scale and customer retention. In the current market, retention is the sharpest predictor of valuation tier — sub-100% NRR triggers punitive discounts, and GRR below 90% signals fundamental product-market fit risk.

Adjustment Methodology

All retention adjustments use continuous linear interpolation between anchor points — every 1% change produces an incremental valuation impact. Curves are asymmetric: penalties below baseline are steeper than premiums above. Formula: Final Multiple = Base Multiple × (1 – Size Discount) × (1 + NRR Adj. + GRR Adj.)
